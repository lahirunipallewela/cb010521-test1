Beaches

Hikkaduwa

Hikkaduwa is very well known as a surfing destination and it annually attracts surfers from most of the countries around the world. There are many good surfing points and although most of them are reef breaks, none of them is risky since they all have a flat deep bottom. The wave size ranges up to 3 metres during the season

Nilaveli

Nilaveli an Open-land of the moon-shine is located north-west of the Island. It is one of the most beautiful beaches in Sri Lanka renowned for its soft white sand and breath-taking bays. It is famous for recreational water sports such as scuba diving.

kalpitiya

Kalpitiya is known as being the best location for Kitesurfing in the country. The summer kitesurfing season is from May to October during the south west monsoon while the winter season is from mid December to mid February during the north east monsoon.
